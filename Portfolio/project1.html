    Inquiry Management System for Enhanced Local Governance

    The Inquiry Management System simplifies how local governments handle public inquiries and requests. It centralizes and automates the process, allowing residents to submit and track their issues while enabling officials to manage and resolve them efficiently.

    With features like real-time status updates and automated notifications, the system improves communication between citizens and local government. It enhances transparency, reduces administrative work, and ensures quicker response times.

    Community Connect

    Community Connect is a platform designed to strengthen engagement between residents and local organizations. It provides tools for users to connect with community events, resources, and services, fostering a more active and informed community.

    The platform features a user-friendly interface for accessing local news, event updates, and support services. By centralizing community information and facilitating communication, Community Connect aims to enhance community participation and streamline access to important resources.
